Academic Information

Content This unit will provide students with the opportunity to gain the hands-on experience necessary to competently conduct FASD assessments in a multidisciplinary or interdisciplinary clinical setting (as per their professional background). This will include observation of assessments, conducting assessments, report writing and taking part in case conferences.
